Theme Colors

Your principal theme color palette includes Primary, Secondary, Dark, Light, and White.

The five principal theme colors generate our theme color utilities, background color utilities, and border color utilities across the framework.

With Natura11y, White is treated as a color and it serves as your default theme color. The white theme is applied automatically to the <body> of pages linked to Naturally's CSS file.
